### 1.1 Core Idea: "Human Genius, Amplified."

VITAL represents a fundamental shift from rigid, fixed capacity to an **elastic, infinite network of intelligence**. We orchestrate expertise, transforming scattered knowledge into compounding structures of insight.

- **Intelligent**: Rational, structured, analytic
- **Minimalist**: Every element intentional
- **Modernist**: Clean geometry, Bauhaus influence
- **Trustworthy**: Professional, confident, premium
- **Humanistic**: Warm modernism, not sterile tech
- **Elastic**: Adaptive, scalable, modular

### 1.3 The VITAL Doctrine

1. **Clarity First** â€” Visual noise removed. Information prioritized.
