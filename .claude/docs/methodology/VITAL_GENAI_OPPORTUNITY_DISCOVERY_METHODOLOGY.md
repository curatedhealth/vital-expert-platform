## Executive Summary

### What This Methodology Delivers

The VITAL GenAI Opportunity Discovery Methodology is a structured approach for identifying, prioritizing, and implementing GenAI solutions within pharmaceutical and life sciences organizations. It combines:

- **Strategic Consulting Frameworks** - Proven 2x2 matrices and prioritization models
- **Enterprise Ontology** - A 7-layer organizational model connecting work to value
- **Platform-Powered Analysis** - Interactive visualization and AI-assisted discovery
- **Value Quantification** - ROI models linking AI investments to business outcomes

### 2. The Push-Pull Dynamic

GenAI opportunities typically fall into two complementary categories:

| Type | Problem | GenAI Solution | Example |
|------|---------|----------------|---------|
| **PULL** | Decision-makers need evidence faster | AI-powered synthesis & Q&A | "What evidence supports market entry in oncology?" |
| **PUSH** | Insights don't reach the right people | Proactive routing & surfacing | Auto-tag reports to upcoming decisions |

**Key Insight:** The most valuable opportunities address BOTH sides simultaneously.

### 3. Strategic Questions Before Platform

Before exploring the platform, establish strategic context with these questions:

| Question | Why It Matters | Discovery Method |
|----------|----------------|------------------|
| What are the top 10 strategic decisions this year? | Focuses on high-value opportunities | Executive interviews |
| Who decides vs. who informs? | Maps RACI and value chain | Org chart analysis |
| What's the cost of slow/wrong decisions? | Quantifies opportunity value | Business case modeling |
| Where is evidence created vs. consumed? | Identifies handoff friction | Process mapping |
| What's "good enough" accuracy for AI? | Defines confidence thresholds | Risk assessment |
