## Quality Standards

Each function's capabilities framework will include:
- ✅ **Taxonomy Document**: ID, Name, Definition, Level Scope, Related Skills
- ✅ **Responsibility Document**: ID, Name, Definition, Level Scope, Accountability
- ✅ **Skill Mapping**: Capability → Skills parent-child hierarchy
- ✅ **SQL Seed Script**: INSERT statements for capabilities/responsibilities
- ✅ **SQL Assignment Script**: Role-based + Level-based assignment logic
- ✅ **Verification Queries**: Coverage and completeness checks
