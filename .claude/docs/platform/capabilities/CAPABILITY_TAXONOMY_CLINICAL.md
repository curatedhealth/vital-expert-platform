## Overview

This document defines **50 capabilities** for Clinical Development & Operations agents:
- **35 role-specific capabilities** targeted to unique clinical roles
- **15 cross-cutting capabilities** shared across roles and levels

## Role-Specific Capabilities (35)

### Leadership & Strategy (4)

**CAP-CD-001: Chief Medical Officer / Chief Development Officer Leadership**
- **Name**: Chief Medical Officer / Chief Development Officer Leadership
- **Definition**: Executive-level clinical development leadership across enterprise, setting development strategy, portfolio management, and ensuring clinical excellence.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER
- **Related Skills**: Clinical Development Strategy, Portfolio Optimization, Executive Leadership, Board Communication

**CAP-CD-002: VP Clinical Development Strategy**
- **Name**: VP Clinical Development Strategy
- **Definition**: Development and execution of clinical development strategy across multiple programs and therapeutic areas with P&L and team accountability.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Development Strategy, Program Management, Budget Leadership, Team Management

**CAP-CD-003: Clinical Development Directorate Management**
- **Name**: Clinical Development Directorate Management
- **Definition**: Leading clinical development for therapeutic area or functional area (operations, data, biostatistics) with team and budget management.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: TA Development Leadership, Functional Leadership, Team Management, Budget Management

**CAP-CD-004: Clinical Program Management**
- **Name**: Clinical Program Management
- **Definition**: Managing clinical development programs from Phase I through approval including strategy, execution, and cross-functional coordination.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Program Planning, Timeline Management, Risk Management, Cross-Functional Leadership

### Protocol Development & Study Design (5)

**CAP-CD-005: Clinical Protocol Development**
- **Name**: Clinical Protocol Development
- **Definition**: Developing comprehensive clinical trial protocols including objectives, design, endpoints, statistical methods, and operational procedures.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Protocol Writing, Study Design, Endpoint Selection, Statistical Planning

**CAP-CD-006: Clinical Study Design & Methodology**
- **Name**: Clinical Study Design & Methodology
- **Definition**: Designing scientifically rigorous clinical trials using appropriate methodologies (RCT, adaptive, basket, umbrella) to answer development questions.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Study Design Expertise, Methodology Selection, Endpoint Strategy, Sample Size Calculation

**CAP-CD-007: Pediatric & Special Population Study Design**
- **Name**: Pediatric & Special Population Study Design
- **Definition**: Designing clinical studies for special populations including pediatrics, elderly, renal/hepatic impairment, and other subgroups.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Pediatric Trial Design, Dose Extrapolation, Special Population Considerations, PK/PD Modeling

**CAP-CD-008: Biomarker & Companion Diagnostic Integration**
- **Name**: Biomarker & Companion Diagnostic Integration
- **Definition**: Integrating biomarkers and companion diagnostics into clinical development programs including validation and regulatory strategy.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Biomarker Strategy, CDx Development, Patient Selection, Regulatory Planning

**CAP-CD-009: Adaptive Trial Design & Implementation**
- **Name**: Adaptive Trial Design & Implementation
- **Definition**: Designing and implementing adaptive clinical trials with planned modifications based on interim analyses while maintaining trial integrity.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Adaptive Design, Interim Analysis, DMC Interaction, Protocol Amendments

---

### Clinical Operations & Execution (6)

**CAP-CD-010: Clinical Trial Management**
- **Name**: Clinical Trial Management
- **Definition**: Managing all operational aspects of clinical trials from start-up through close-out ensuring quality, timeline, and budget adherence.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Trial Management, Vendor Management, Budget Tracking, Timeline Management

**CAP-CD-011: Site Identification & Selection**
- **Name**: Site Identification & Selection
- **Definition**: Identifying, evaluating, and selecting clinical trial sites based on feasibility, capacity, quality, and enrollment potential.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Site Feasibility, Site Qualification, Investigator Assessment, Geographic Strategy

**CAP-CD-012: Site Initiation & Start-Up**
- **Name**: Site Initiation & Start-Up
- **Definition**: Managing site initiation activities including regulatory submissions, contract negotiation, training, and site initiation visits.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: SIV Conduct, Site Training, Regulatory Start-Up, Contract Negotiation

**CAP-CD-013: Clinical Site Monitoring (CRA)**
- **Name**: Clinical Site Monitoring (CRA)
- **Definition**: Conducting on-site and remote monitoring to ensure protocol compliance, data quality, patient safety, and GCP adherence.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Source Data Verification, Protocol Compliance Checking, GCP Monitoring, Issue Resolution

**CAP-CD-014: Patient Recruitment & Enrollment**
- **Name**: Patient Recruitment & Enrollment
- **Definition**: Developing and executing patient recruitment strategies to achieve enrollment targets within timeline and ensure diverse patient populations.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Recruitment Strategy, Enrollment Forecasting, Patient Outreach, Retention Planning

**CAP-CD-015: Clinical Supply Management**
- **Name**: Clinical Supply Management
- **Definition**: Managing clinical trial supplies including forecasting, distribution, accountability, and reconciliation ensuring no stockouts or patient delays.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Supply Forecasting, Distribution Planning, Drug Accountability, Reconciliation

### Biostatistics & Statistical Programming (4)

**CAP-CD-020: Biostatistics Strategy & Planning**
- **Name**: Biostatistics Strategy & Planning
- **Definition**: Developing biostatistics strategy for clinical development programs including analysis approaches, adaptive methods, and sample size optimization.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Statistical Strategy, Sample Size Calculation, Power Analysis, Statistical Methodology

**CAP-CD-021: Statistical Analysis Plan Development**
- **Name**: Statistical Analysis Plan Development
- **Definition**: Developing comprehensive statistical analysis plans (SAPs) defining all analyses, handling of missing data, and sensitivity analyses.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: SAP Writing, Analysis Specification, Missing Data Strategy, Sensitivity Analysis

**CAP-CD-022: Clinical Trial Data Analysis**
- **Name**: Clinical Trial Data Analysis
- **Definition**: Conducting statistical analyses of clinical trial data including efficacy, safety, and exploratory analyses per SAP.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Statistical Analysis (SAS/R), Efficacy Analysis, Safety Analysis, TFLs Generation

**CAP-CD-023: Data Monitoring Committee Support**
- **Name**: Data Monitoring Committee Support
- **Definition**: Supporting Data Monitoring Committees (DMC/DSMB) including preparation of interim analyses, safety reports, and recommendations.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Interim Analysis, DMC Report Preparation, Unblinding Procedures, Safety Analysis
