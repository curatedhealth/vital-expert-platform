## Role-Specific Capabilities (28)

### Leadership & Strategy (4)

**CAP-CM-001: Chief Commercial Officer Leadership**
- **Definition**: Executive commercial leadership including strategy, P&L, brand portfolio, sales force, and market performance.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER

**CAP-CM-002: Commercial Strategy & Planning**
- **Definition**: Developing commercial strategy including market segmentation, positioning, pricing, and go-to-market approach.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT

**CAP-CM-003: Brand Leadership & Portfolio Management**
- **Definition**: Leading brand teams and managing product portfolio including prioritization, investment, and lifecycle management.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-CM-004: Sales Leadership & Field Management**
- **Definition**: Leading sales organization including strategy, force sizing, territory design, and performance management.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT
