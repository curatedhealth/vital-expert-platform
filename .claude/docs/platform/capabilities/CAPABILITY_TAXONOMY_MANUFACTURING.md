## Overview

---

## Role-Specific Capabilities (32)

### Leadership & Strategy (3)

**CAP-MF-001: VP Manufacturing & CMC Leadership**
- **Definition**: Executive leadership for manufacturing operations and CMC strategy across drug substance and drug product.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER

**CAP-MF-002: CMC Strategic Leadership**
- **Definition**: Leading CMC development strategy from early development through commercialization including regulatory CMC.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MF-003: Manufacturing Site Leadership**
- **Definition**: Leading manufacturing site operations including production, quality, compliance, and continuous improvement.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT

---

### Process Development & Optimization (6)

**CAP-MF-004: Process Development Strategy**
- **Definition**: Developing process development strategy for drug substance and drug product including platform approaches.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MF-005: Chemical Synthesis & Process Chemistry**
- **Definition**: Developing chemical synthesis routes and optimizing process chemistry for API manufacturing.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-006: Biologics Process Development**
- **Definition**: Developing upstream and downstream processes for biologics including cell line development and purification.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-007: Formulation Development**
- **Definition**: Developing pharmaceutical formulations including excipient selection, stability, and manufacturability.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-008: Process Optimization & Robustness**
- **Definition**: Optimizing manufacturing processes for yield, quality, and robustness using DOE and statistical methods.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-009: Continuous Manufacturing & PAT**
- **Definition**: Implementing continuous manufacturing and process analytical technology for real-time quality control.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

---

### Analytical Development (4)

**CAP-MF-010: Analytical Method Development**
- **Definition**: Developing analytical methods for identity, purity, potency, and stability testing per ICH guidelines.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-011: Method Validation & Transfer**
- **Definition**: Validating analytical methods and transferring to QC laboratories per ICH Q2(R1) guidelines.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-012: Stability Program Development**
- **Definition**: Designing and executing stability programs including real-time, accelerated, and stress studies.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-013: Characterization & Comparability**
- **Definition**: Characterizing drug substance/product and conducting comparability assessments for process changes.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

---

### Scale-Up & Technology Transfer (4)

**CAP-MF-014: Process Scale-Up**
- **Definition**: Scaling manufacturing processes from lab to pilot to commercial scale maintaining quality and performance.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-015: Technology Transfer Management**
- **Definition**: Managing technology transfers between sites including documentation, training, and validation.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-016: Manufacturing Readiness & Validation**
- **Definition**: Ensuring manufacturing readiness including process validation, equipment qualification, and facility readiness.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MF-017: Comparability Protocol Development**
- **Definition**: Developing comparability protocols for process changes including analytical strategy and acceptance criteria.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

---

### GMP Manufacturing Operations (5)

**CAP-MF-018: GMP Production Management**
- **Definition**: Managing GMP manufacturing operations ensuring compliance with cGMP, GDP, and quality standards.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-019: Batch Manufacturing & Execution**
- **Definition**: Executing batch manufacturing per batch records ensuring quality, yield, and compliance.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-020: Manufacturing Equipment Operations**
- **Definition**: Operating pharmaceutical manufacturing equipment including reactors, fermenters, filling lines, and packaging.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST, L4 WORKER

**CAP-MF-021: Batch Record Review & Release**
- **Definition**: Reviewing batch records for compliance, investigating deviations, and authorizing batch release.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-022: Manufacturing Troubleshooting**
- **Definition**: Troubleshooting manufacturing issues including out-of-specification results, equipment failures, and yield issues.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

---

### Quality Control & Assurance (4)

**CAP-MF-023: Quality Control Testing**
- **Definition**: Conducting quality control testing including release testing, stability testing, and raw material testing.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-024: Quality Assurance & Compliance**
- **Definition**: Ensuring quality assurance including deviation management, CAPA, change control, and audit readiness.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-025: GMP Inspection Readiness**
- **Definition**: Preparing for and managing GMP inspections including FDA, EMA, and other regulatory authorities.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MF-026: Quality Risk Management (ICH Q9)**
- **Definition**: Conducting quality risk assessments using FMEA, HACCP, and other QRM tools per ICH Q9.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

---

### Supply Chain & Planning (3)

**CAP-MF-027: Manufacturing Planning & Scheduling**
- **Definition**: Planning and scheduling manufacturing campaigns balancing demand, capacity, and inventory.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-028: Supply Chain Management (Manufacturing)**
- **Definition**: Managing pharmaceutical supply chain including raw materials, API, and finished goods.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MF-029: Inventory Management & Logistics**
- **Definition**: Managing inventory including warehousing, cold chain, and distribution logistics.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

---

### CMC Regulatory (3)

**CAP-MF-030: CMC Regulatory Strategy & Submissions**
- **Definition**: Developing CMC regulatory strategy and preparing Module 3 documentation for submissions.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MF-031: Manufacturing Site Registration**
- **Definition**: Managing facility registrations, site master files, and manufacturing authorizations.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MF-032: Post-Approval CMC Changes**
- **Definition**: Managing post-approval CMC changes including comparability, validation, and regulatory submissions.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
