## Overview

**45 capabilities** for Market Access & HEOR agents:
- **30 role-specific capabilities**
- **15 cross-cutting capabilities**

---

## Role-Specific Capabilities (30)

### Leadership & Strategy (3)

**CAP-MA-001: VP Market Access & Reimbursement Leadership**
- **Definition**: Executive leadership for market access strategy, payer engagement, pricing, and reimbursement across markets.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER

**CAP-MA-002: Global Market Access Strategy**
- **Definition**: Developing and executing global market access strategy including value proposition, evidence generation, and payer engagement.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-003: HEOR Strategic Leadership**
- **Definition**: Leading health economics and outcomes research function including strategy, evidence generation, and value demonstration.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

---

### Payer Engagement & Contracting (5)

**CAP-MA-004: Payer Account Management**
- **Definition**: Managing relationships with national and regional payers including health plans, PBMs, and integrated delivery networks.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-005: Payer Negotiation & Contracting**
- **Definition**: Negotiating contracts with payers including formulary placement, pricing, rebates, and utilization management.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-006: Formulary Strategy & Management**
- **Definition**: Developing formulary strategies and managing formulary submissions, P&T presentations, and tier placement.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-007: Reimbursement & Coding Strategy**
- **Definition**: Developing reimbursement strategies including coding (ICD/CPT/HCPCS), coverage policies, and payment models.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-008: Value-Based Contracting**
- **Definition**: Designing and implementing value-based contracts including outcomes-based agreements and risk-sharing arrangements.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

---

### Health Economics & Modeling (6)

**CAP-MA-009: Economic Modeling & Analysis**
- **Definition**: Developing health economic models including cost-effectiveness, cost-utility, and budget impact analyses.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-010: Cost-Effectiveness Analysis (CEA)**
- **Definition**: Conducting cost-effectiveness analyses comparing interventions using incremental cost-effectiveness ratios (ICERs).
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-011: Budget Impact Analysis (BIA)**
- **Definition**: Developing budget impact models to estimate financial impact of product adoption on payer budgets.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-012: Pharmacoeconomic Modeling**
- **Definition**: Building decision-analytic models (decision trees, Markov, discrete event simulation) for economic evaluation.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-013: Health Technology Assessment (HTA)**
- **Definition**: Preparing HTA submissions for agencies (NICE, CADTH, IQWIG, HAS) and defending economic evaluations.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-014: Economic Model Validation**
- **Definition**: Validating economic models for technical accuracy, face validity, and compliance with guidelines (ISPOR, NICE).
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

---

### Real-World Evidence & Outcomes (5)

**CAP-MA-015: Real-World Evidence Strategy**
- **Definition**: Developing RWE strategy to generate evidence for market access including study design and data sources.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-016: Observational Study Design (RWE)**
- **Definition**: Designing observational studies using claims, registry, and EHR data to assess effectiveness and outcomes.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-017: Patient-Reported Outcomes (PRO)**
- **Definition**: Selecting, validating, and analyzing patient-reported outcomes to support value propositions.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-018: Real-World Data Analysis**
- **Definition**: Analyzing real-world data using epidemiological and statistical methods to generate evidence.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-019: Comparative Effectiveness Research**
- **Definition**: Conducting comparative effectiveness research comparing interventions using real-world or trial data.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

---

### Pricing & Value Communication (5)

**CAP-MA-020: Global Pricing Strategy**
- **Definition**: Developing global pricing strategy including launch pricing, reference pricing, and price optimization.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-021: Value Proposition Development**
- **Definition**: Developing compelling value propositions for payers, providers, and patients based on clinical and economic evidence.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-022: Value Dossier Development**
- **Definition**: Creating comprehensive value dossiers including evidence summary, economic evaluation, and budget impact.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-023: Health Economics Communication**
- **Definition**: Communicating health economic data and value messages to payers, P&T committees, and stakeholders.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-024: Market Access Evidence Generation**
- **Definition**: Identifying evidence gaps and generating targeted evidence to support market access and reimbursement.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT

---

### Policy & Access (3)

**CAP-MA-025: Health Policy & Advocacy**
- **Definition**: Engaging with health policy including policy analysis, advocacy, and shaping reimbursement policies.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT

**CAP-MA-026: Coverage Policy Development**
- **Definition**: Developing and influencing coverage policies including local and national coverage determinations.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-027: Patient Access Programs**
- **Definition**: Designing and managing patient access programs including co-pay assistance, patient assistance, and hub services.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST

---

### Market Access Operations (3)

**CAP-MA-028: Market Access Analytics**
- **Definition**: Analyzing market access data including formulary status, coverage, utilization, and competitive positioning.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-029: Payer Data & Intelligence**
- **Definition**: Gathering and analyzing payer intelligence including policies, trends, and decision-making criteria.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST

**CAP-MA-030: Pull-Through & Access Monitoring**
- **Definition**: Monitoring product access and pull-through including utilization, denials, and access barriers.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
