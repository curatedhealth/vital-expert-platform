**Version**: 1.0

## Overview

This document defines **40 capabilities** for Safety & Pharmacovigilance agents:
- **28 role-specific capabilities** targeted to unique safety roles
- **12 cross-cutting capabilities** shared across roles and levels

---

## Role-Specific Capabilities (28)

### Leadership & Strategy (3)

**CAP-SV-001: Chief Safety Officer / VP Pharmacovigilance Leadership**
- **Name**: Chief Safety Officer / VP Pharmacovigilance Leadership
- **Definition**: Executive-level safety leadership across enterprise, setting global pharmacovigilance strategy, ensuring patient safety, and managing safety function.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER
- **Related Skills**: Global Safety Strategy, Patient Safety Leadership, Risk Management, Regulatory Compliance

**CAP-SV-002: Global Safety Leadership**
- **Name**: Global Safety Leadership
- **Definition**: Leading global pharmacovigilance operations including case management, signal detection, regulatory reporting, and safety systems.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: PV Operations Management, Global Safety Strategy, Team Leadership, Budget Management

**CAP-SV-003: Safety Medical Direction**
- **Name**: Safety Medical Direction
- **Definition**: Providing medical leadership for safety function including benefit-risk assessment, signal evaluation, and regulatory safety strategy.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Medical Safety Leadership, Benefit-Risk Assessment, Signal Evaluation, Medical Writing

---

### Case Management & Processing (4)

**CAP-SV-004: Adverse Event Case Processing**
- **Name**: Adverse Event Case Processing
- **Definition**: Processing individual case safety reports (ICSRs) including data entry, medical review, coding, and quality control.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST, L4 WORKER
- **Related Skills**: Case Processing, MedDRA Coding, Data Entry, WHO-DD Coding

**CAP-SV-005: Serious Adverse Event Assessment**
- **Name**: Serious Adverse Event Assessment
- **Definition**: Assessing serious adverse events for seriousness criteria, causality, expectedness, and reportability per regulatory timelines.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: SAE Assessment, Causality Determination, Expectedness Evaluation, Regulatory Timelines

**CAP-SV-006: Expedited Safety Reporting**
- **Name**: Expedited Safety Reporting
- **Definition**: Managing expedited safety reports (15-day/7-day) to health authorities ensuring compliance with reporting timelines and quality standards.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Expedited Reporting, Regulatory Submission, Timeline Management, Quality Review

**CAP-SV-007: Safety Data Management & Quality**
- **Name**: Safety Data Management & Quality
- **Definition**: Managing safety databases, ensuring data quality, reconciliation, and maintaining validated safety systems.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Safety Database Management, Data Quality, Reconciliation, System Validation

---

### Signal Detection & Risk Management (5)

**CAP-SV-008: Signal Detection & Data Mining**
- **Name**: Signal Detection & Data Mining
- **Definition**: Conducting signal detection using statistical methods, data mining, and literature surveillance to identify potential safety signals.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Statistical Signal Detection, Data Mining, Disproportionality Analysis, Literature Monitoring

**CAP-SV-009: Signal Evaluation & Assessment**
- **Name**: Signal Evaluation & Assessment
- **Definition**: Evaluating detected signals for clinical significance, causality, and need for regulatory action or risk mitigation.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Signal Assessment, Clinical Evaluation, Causality Analysis, Risk Characterization

**CAP-SV-010: Risk Management Planning (Safety)**
- **Name**: Risk Management Planning (Safety)
- **Definition**: Developing and implementing risk management plans including risk minimization measures and effectiveness monitoring.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: RMP Development, Risk Minimization, REMS Design, Effectiveness Assessment

**CAP-SV-011: Benefit-Risk Management**
- **Name**: Benefit-Risk Management
- **Definition**: Conducting ongoing benefit-risk assessments throughout product lifecycle to inform safety decisions and regulatory actions.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Benefit-Risk Analysis, Risk Characterization, Benefit Quantification, Decision-Making

**CAP-SV-012: Safety Signal Management Committees**
- **Name**: Safety Signal Management Committees
- **Definition**: Managing safety signal committees including agenda setting, presentation preparation, and implementation of recommendations.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Committee Management, Presentation Development, Recommendation Implementation, Documentation

### Aggregate Reporting & Analysis (4)

**CAP-SV-013: PSUR/PBRER Development**
- **Name**: PSUR/PBRER Development
- **Definition**: Preparing Periodic Safety Update Reports (PSURs) and Periodic Benefit-Risk Evaluation Reports (PBRERs) per regulatory requirements.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: PSUR Writing, Data Analysis, Benefit-Risk Assessment, Regulatory Formatting

**CAP-SV-014: Development Safety Update Reports (DSUR)**
- **Name**: Development Safety Update Reports (DSUR)
- **Definition**: Preparing annual Development Safety Update Reports for investigational products per ICH E2F guidelines.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: DSUR Preparation, Clinical Development Safety, Data Integration, ICH E2F

**CAP-SV-015: Safety Data Analysis & Epidemiology**
- **Name**: Safety Data Analysis & Epidemiology
- **Definition**: Conducting epidemiological analyses of safety data including incidence rates, risk factors, and comparative safety.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Related Skills**: Epidemiological Analysis, Statistical Methods, Data Interpretation, Risk Estimation

**CAP-SV-016: Aggregate Safety Report Authoring**
- **Name**: Aggregate Safety Report Authoring
- **Definition**: Authoring aggregate safety reports including PASRs, Annual Safety Reports, and regulatory safety sections.
- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: Medical Writing, Safety Data Synthesis, Report Authoring, Regulatory Standards

- **Level Scope**: L3 SPECIALIST
- **Related Skills**: PSMF Maintenance, Documentation, System Description, EU Requirements

**CAP-SV-020: Safety Inspections & Audits**

- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
