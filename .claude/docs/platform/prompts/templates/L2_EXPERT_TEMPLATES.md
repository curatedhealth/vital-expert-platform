# L2 Expert Prompt Starter Templates

## Overview

L2 Experts are strategic specialists who lead complex initiatives, develop comprehensive strategies, and provide senior-level guidance across their domain.

## Template Patterns

### Strategy Development
```
"Develop a comprehensive [domain] strategy for [context/product/initiative] including [component 1], [component 2], and [component 3]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[domain]`: medical affairs, regulatory, commercial, clinical development
- `[context]`: product launch, therapeutic area, pipeline asset
- `[components]`: KOL engagement, evidence generation, publication planning, MSL deployment

**Examples:**
```
"Develop a comprehensive medical affairs strategy for our oncology portfolio launch including KOL mapping, publication planning, and MSL deployment priorities"

"Develop a comprehensive regulatory strategy for our accelerated approval pathway including FDA engagement, post-marketing commitments, and label optimization"
```

---

### Framework/Model Creation
```
"Create a [framework/model/system] for [purpose] that [achieves outcome] while [addressing constraint]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[framework type]`: value proposition framework, governance model, operating model, excellence framework
- `[purpose]`: demonstrating ROI, ensuring compliance, optimizing resources
- `[outcome]`: measurable impact, organizational capability, stakeholder alignment
- `[constraint]`: regulatory compliance, budget constraints, timeline requirements

**Examples:**
```
"Create a medical affairs value proposition framework demonstrating our contribution to clinical development, commercial success, and patient outcomes"

"Create an evidence generation governance model ensuring scientific rigor while meeting commercial timeline requirements"
```

---

### Program Design
```
"Design a [program/initiative] for [target audience] addressing [objectives] through [methods/approaches]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[program type]`: medical education program, KOL development program, MSL training program
- `[target audience]`: healthcare professionals, investigators, internal teams
- `[objectives]`: knowledge gaps, behavior change, scientific engagement
- `[methods]`: CME activities, advisory boards, digital platforms

**Examples:**
```
"Design a comprehensive continuing medical education strategy for our therapeutic area including needs assessment, curriculum development, and outcome measurement"

"Design a KOL development program identifying emerging thought leaders and building long-term scientific relationships"
```

---

### Evaluation/Assessment
```
"Evaluate our current [function/process/program] and recommend [improvements/enhancements] to [achieve goal]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[subject]`: medical affairs organizational structure, publication portfolio, MSL effectiveness
- `[improvements]`: process enhancements, capability upgrades, resource optimization
- `[goal]`: support pipeline growth, maximize impact, improve efficiency

**Examples:**
```
"Evaluate our current medical affairs organizational structure and recommend enhancements to support pipeline growth and geographic expansion"

"Evaluate our publication portfolio against competitive landscape and recommend gap-filling manuscripts"
```

---

### Roadmap/Planning
```
"Build a [roadmap/plan] for [timeframe/scope] with [elements] to [demonstrate/achieve] [outcome]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[roadmap type]`: evidence generation roadmap, publication plan, engagement strategy
- `[timeframe]`: 12-month, pre-launch, post-approval
- `[elements]`: milestones, KPIs, resource requirements
- `[outcome]`: payer evidence gaps, regulatory commitments, competitive differentiation

**Examples:**
```
"Build a post-launch evidence generation roadmap addressing remaining clinical questions, payer evidence gaps, and competitive differentiation needs"

"Build a medical affairs budget model with KPIs and metrics to demonstrate return on medical investment to senior leadership"
```

## Medical Affairs L2 Expert Templates

### Medical Affairs Strategist
1. "Develop a comprehensive integrated medical affairs strategy for our upcoming [therapeutic area] launch, including KOL engagement, evidence generation, and publication planning"
2. "Create a medical affairs value proposition framework demonstrating our contribution to clinical development, commercial success, and patient outcomes"
3. "Design a post-launch evidence generation roadmap addressing remaining clinical questions, payer evidence gaps, and competitive differentiation needs"
4. "Evaluate our current medical affairs organizational structure and recommend enhancements to support pipeline growth and geographic expansion"
5. "Build a medical affairs budget model with KPIs and metrics to demonstrate return on medical investment to senior leadership"

### Medical Education Director
1. "Design a comprehensive continuing medical education (CME) strategy for our therapeutic area, including needs assessment, curriculum development, and outcome measurement"
2. "Develop an innovative digital medical education platform strategy incorporating microlearning, case-based modules, and interactive simulations"
3. "Create a healthcare professional education pathway addressing knowledge gaps from disease awareness through treatment optimization"
4. "Evaluate our medical education program effectiveness using learning outcomes, behavior change metrics, and patient impact measures"
5. "Build a global medical education governance framework ensuring scientific accuracy, compliance, and consistent quality across regions"

### Regional Medical Director
1. "Develop a regional medical affairs strategy aligned with global medical plans while addressing local market needs and regulatory requirements"
2. "Create an MSL deployment and territory optimization plan maximizing coverage of key accounts and opinion leaders"
3. "Design a regional KOL engagement program identifying, prioritizing, and developing relationships with influential healthcare professionals"
4. "Build a regional medical affairs operating model addressing resource allocation, governance, and cross-functional collaboration"
5. "Evaluate regional competitive landscape and recommend differentiated medical engagement strategies"

### Publication Strategy Lead
1. "Develop a comprehensive publication strategy for our clinical development program from Phase 1 through post-marketing, prioritizing key data disclosures"
2. "Create a congress strategy for the upcoming major medical meeting including abstract submissions, poster presentations, and symposium planning"
3. "Design a real-world evidence publication program to address remaining clinical questions and support market access objectives"
4. "Evaluate our publication portfolio against competitive landscape and recommend gap-filling manuscripts"
5. "Build a publication governance framework ensuring ICMJE compliance, author engagement, and timely disclosure"

## Quality Checklist for L2 Starters

- [ ] 120-200+ characters
- [ ] Strategic/comprehensive scope
- [ ] Multiple components or considerations
- [ ] Clear leadership-level deliverable
- [ ] Aligned with expert responsibilities
- [ ] Uses strategic vocabulary (develop, design, create, build, evaluate)
