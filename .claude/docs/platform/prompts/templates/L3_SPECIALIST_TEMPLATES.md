# L3 Specialist Prompt Starter Templates

## Overview

L3 Specialists are domain experts who execute focused tasks, create specific deliverables, and apply specialized expertise within their functional area.

## Template Patterns

### Document Drafting
```
"Draft a [document type] for [purpose/audience] including [key elements]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[document type]`: clinical study report, medical inquiry response, KOL engagement plan, publication manuscript
- `[purpose]`: regulatory submission, HCP question, advisory board, journal submission
- `[key elements]`: required sections, specific data, compliance elements

**Examples:**
```
"Draft a clinical study report following ICH E3 guidelines for our completed Phase 2 study"
"Draft a medical inquiry response to an HCP question about off-label use with appropriate disclaimers and references"
```

---

### Analysis/Evaluation
```
"Analyze the [data/subject] to identify [insights/findings] for [use case]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[data/subject]`: clinical trial data, safety signals, competitive landscape, KOL profiles
- `[insights]`: trends, risks, opportunities, gaps
- `[use case]`: regulatory submission, strategic planning, publication, decision support

**Examples:**
```
"Analyze the primary efficacy endpoint data from our Phase 3 study and generate summary statistics with confidence intervals"
"Analyze biomarker data from our Phase 1 study to identify potential responder subgroups"
```

---

### Preparation/Planning
```
"Prepare a [deliverable] for [event/purpose] addressing [requirements]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[deliverable]`: slide deck, briefing document, meeting materials, training content
- `[event/purpose]`: advisory board, KOL meeting, FDA meeting, internal review
- `[requirements]`: scientific objectives, compliance, stakeholder needs

**Examples:**
```
"Prepare scientific talking points and slide deck for an upcoming investigator meeting"
"Prepare pre-reading materials and discussion guides for advisory board participants"
```

---

### Review/Validation
```
"Review [content/data] for [quality criteria] and [action required]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[content]`: manuscript draft, promotional materials, clinical data, safety reports
- `[quality criteria]`: scientific accuracy, regulatory compliance, ICMJE guidelines
- `[action]`: flag issues, provide recommendations, approve/reject

**Examples:**
```
"Review promotional materials for scientific accuracy and appropriate claims substantiation"
"Review our biomarker development package for FDA/EMA regulatory submission readiness"
```

---

### Creation/Development
```
"Create a [output type] summarizing/presenting [information] for [audience/purpose]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[output type]`: KOL profile, data visualization, response template, training module
- `[information]`: clinical data, competitive intelligence, safety findings
- `[audience]`: MSL team, advisory board, HCPs, internal stakeholders

**Examples:**
```
"Create a KOL profile for the top 20 thought leaders in our therapeutic area"
"Create data visualizations (forest plots, waterfall plots) for the upcoming publication manuscript"
```

---

### Tracking/Coordination
```
"Track [items/activities] and [report/flag] [status/issues] for [stakeholders]"
```

**Variables:**
- `[items]`: project milestones, review approvals, enrollment, submissions
- `[report type]`: status update, variance report, compliance report
- `[stakeholders]`: management, cross-functional teams, regulatory authorities

**Examples:**
```
"Track publication milestones including manuscript drafts, author reviews, and journal submissions"
"Track and report KOL engagement activities including meeting summaries, insights captured, and follow-up actions"
```

## Medical Affairs L3 Specialist Templates

### Medical Writer
1. "Draft a clinical study report (CSR) following ICH E3 guidelines for our completed Phase 2 study"
2. "Write an investigator brochure update incorporating new safety and efficacy data"
3. "Develop a manuscript draft for submission to a peer-reviewed journal following ICMJE guidelines"
4. "Create a plain language summary of clinical trial results for patient and public audiences"
5. "Draft a scientific poster abstract for submission to an upcoming medical congress"

### Medical Science Liaison Advisor
1. "Develop a scientific engagement strategy for a high-priority KOL considering their research interests and clinical practice"
2. "Create a territory business plan with prioritized accounts, engagement objectives, and resource allocation"
3. "Prepare scientific talking points and slide deck for an upcoming investigator meeting"
4. "Draft a field medical insight report capturing HCP feedback on unmet needs and treatment challenges"
5. "Review clinical data to develop compelling scientific narratives for peer-to-peer discussions"

### Clinical Trial Designer
1. "Design a Phase 2 dose-finding study using an adaptive design to optimize dose selection efficiency"
2. "Develop inclusion/exclusion criteria that balance scientific rigor with enrollment feasibility"
3. "Create a study schema with visit schedules, assessment windows, and procedure timing"
4. "Evaluate basket trial vs umbrella trial designs for our oncology program"
5. "Recommend statistical design parameters including sample size, power, and interim analysis strategy"

### Safety Signal Evaluator
1. "Evaluate a potential safety signal identified through disproportionality analysis of our post-marketing data"
2. "Conduct a cumulative review of hepatotoxicity cases to assess signal strength and clinical significance"
3. "Prepare a signal evaluation report for Safety Management Team review with risk assessment and recommendations"
4. "Analyze clinical trial safety data to identify emerging safety trends requiring further investigation"
5. "Review published literature for reports of similar adverse events with our drug class"

### Medical Information Specialist
1. "Draft a medical information response to an HCP inquiry about off-label use with appropriate disclaimers"
2. "Create a standard response document for frequently asked questions about our product safety profile"
3. "Develop a medical information request tracking system with response time metrics and quality indicators"
4. "Review and update our medical information database with latest clinical data and labeling changes"
5. "Prepare a literature summary responding to a complex clinical question requiring comprehensive evidence review"

## Quality Checklist for L3 Starters

- [ ] 80-150+ characters
- [ ] Single focused task/deliverable
- [ ] Domain-specific terminology
- [ ] Clear expected output
- [ ] Aligned with specialist responsibilities
- [ ] Uses action verbs (draft, analyze, prepare, review, create, track)
