**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Date**: November 26, 2025  
**Status**: In Progress

---

## Structure

Each responsibility includes:
- **ID**: Unique identifier (e.g., RESP-MA-001)
- **Name**: Responsibility name
- **Definition**: Clear description of the responsibility and expected outcomes
- **Level Scope**: Which agent levels this applies to (L1/L2/L3/L4/L5)
- **Roles**: Specific roles that own this responsibility
- **Accountability**: What success looks like

## LEADERSHIP & STRATEGIC RESPONSIBILITIES (C-Suite, VP, Director)

**RESP-MA-001: Enterprise Medical Strategy & Vision**
- **Name**: Enterprise Medical Strategy & Vision
- **Definition**: Own and execute enterprise-wide medical strategy across all therapeutic areas, geographies, and stakeholders. Set medical vision, priorities, and resource allocation at highest level.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER
- **Roles**: Chief Medical Officer
- **Accountability**: Enterprise medical objectives achieved, board-level influence, external reputation, organizational medical capability

**RESP-MA-002: Regional/Global Medical Portfolio Leadership**
- **Name**: Regional/Global Medical Portfolio Leadership  
- **Definition**: Lead medical affairs for regional/global portfolio including budget ownership, team leadership across multiple functions, and strategic objective delivery.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER
- **Roles**: VP Medical Affairs
- **Accountability**: Portfolio KPIs met, budget targets achieved, team performance, stakeholder alignment

**RESP-MA-003: Medical Function/TA Leadership**
- **Name**: Medical Function/Therapeutic Area Leadership
- **Definition**: Direct leadership of medical affairs function or therapeutic area including team management, budget oversight, and delivery of strategic medical initiatives.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT
- **Roles**: Medical Affairs Director, Senior Medical Director, Field Medical Director
- **Accountability**: Function goals achieved, team development, budget adherence, cross-functional relationships

**RESP-MA-004: Medical Governance Framework Ownership**
- **Name**: Medical Governance Framework Ownership
- **Definition**: Establish, maintain, and enforce medical governance frameworks, compliance programs, quality standards, and audit processes across medical affairs.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Roles**: Medical Governance Officer, Medical Excellence Lead
- **Accountability**: Zero critical audit findings, compliance training completion, governance adherence, continuous improvement
