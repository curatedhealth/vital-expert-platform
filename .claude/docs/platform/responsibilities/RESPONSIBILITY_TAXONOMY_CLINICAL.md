## Role-Specific Responsibilities (35)

### Leadership & Strategy (4)

**RESP-CD-001: Clinical Development Strategy Execution**
- **Definition**: Own and execute clinical development strategy achieving development milestones, regulatory approvals, and budget targets.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER
- **Accountability**: Development plan completion (100%), approval timelines, P&L performance, strategic objectives

**RESP-CD-002: Clinical Program Delivery**
- **Definition**: Deliver clinical programs from Phase I through approval achieving quality, timeline, and budget objectives.
- **Level Scope**: L1 MASTER, L2 EXPERT
- **Accountability**: Program milestones (>90%), approval success, budget adherence (±5%), quality standards

**RESP-CD-003: Functional Leadership & Team Management**
- **Definition**: Lead clinical functional area (operations, data, biostatistics) with team performance and budget accountability.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT
- **Accountability**: Team performance, budget management, capability development, operational excellence

**RESP-CD-004: Clinical Trial Success Delivery**
- **Definition**: Deliver successful clinical trials meeting enrollment, timeline, quality, and data objectives.
- **Level Scope**: L2 EXPERT, L3 SPECIALIST
- **Accountability**: Enrollment targets (>90%), timeline adherence (>85%), data quality (>98%), budget adherence
