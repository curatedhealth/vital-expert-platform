**RESP-CM-022: Digital Marketing ROI** - Achieve >200% digital marketing ROI with >80% engagement rates.

**RESP-CM-023: Speaker Program Quality** - Deliver speaker programs achieving >85% attendee satisfaction and behavior change.

**RESP-CM-024: Commercial Analytics Accuracy** - Deliver analytics with >95% forecast accuracy (±10%) and actionable insights.
