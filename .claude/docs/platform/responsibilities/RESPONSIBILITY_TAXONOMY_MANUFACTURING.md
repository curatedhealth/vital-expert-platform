# Manufacturing & CMC Responsibility Taxonomy
**Version**: 1.0 | **Date**: November 27, 2025 | **Status**: COMPLETE

## Role-Specific Responsibilities (32)

**RESP-MF-001: Manufacturing Operations Excellence** - Achieve manufacturing excellence with >95% OEE, zero critical quality issues, and 100% GMP compliance.

**RESP-MF-002: CMC Development Strategy Success** - Execute CMC strategy achieving development milestones, regulatory approvals, and manufacturing readiness.

**RESP-MF-003: Site Operations Performance** - Achieve site operations targets including production, quality, safety, and financial performance.

**RESP-MF-004: Process Development Excellence** - Develop robust processes achieving >95% success rate in scale-up and technology transfer.

**RESP-MF-005: Chemical Synthesis Optimization** - Optimize synthesis achieving >85% yield, >98% purity, and commercial viability.

**RESP-MF-006: Biologics Process Performance** - Achieve biologics process targets including titer, purity (>95%), and recovery (>80%).

**RESP-MF-007: Formulation Success** - Develop formulations achieving stability, manufacturability, and regulatory acceptance (>90%).

**RESP-MF-008: Process Robustness** - Demonstrate process robustness with >95% capability (Cpk >1.33) and minimal variability.

**RESP-MF-009: Continuous Manufacturing Implementation** - Implement continuous manufacturing achieving >90% productivity gains and real-time quality.

**RESP-MF-010: Analytical Method Validation** - Validate methods achieving 100% ICH compliance and regulatory acceptance.

**RESP-MF-011: Method Transfer Success** - Transfer methods achieving >95% success rate and equivalency demonstration.

**RESP-MF-012: Stability Program Compliance** - Maintain stability programs with 100% regulatory compliance and zero specification failures.

**RESP-MF-013: Characterization Completeness** - Complete characterization with 100% regulatory adequacy and comparability success.

**RESP-MF-014: Scale-Up Success Rate** - Achieve >90% scale-up success maintaining quality and performance across scales.

**RESP-MF-015: Technology Transfer Excellence** - Execute tech transfers with >95% success rate and minimal timeline impact.

**RESP-MF-016: Manufacturing Validation Success** - Complete process validation with 100% regulatory acceptance and first-time approval.

**RESP-MF-017: Comparability Demonstration** - Demonstrate comparability achieving 100% regulatory acceptance and zero approval delays.

**RESP-MF-018: GMP Compliance Achievement** - Maintain 100% GMP compliance with zero critical deviations and >95% first-pass yield.

**RESP-MF-019: Batch Success Rate** - Achieve >98% batch success rate with zero product recalls and >95% quality acceptance.

**RESP-MF-020: Equipment Performance** - Maintain equipment performance with >95% OEE, <5% unplanned downtime, and zero safety incidents.

**RESP-MF-021: Batch Release Timeliness** - Release batches within target timelines (>95% on-time) with zero quality hold extensions.

**RESP-MF-022: Manufacturing Issue Resolution** - Resolve manufacturing issues achieving >90% first-time fix rate and minimal production impact.

**RESP-MF-023: QC Testing Timeliness** - Complete QC testing within SLA (>95%) with >98% first-time pass rate.

**RESP-MF-024: Quality System Effectiveness** - Maintain quality system achieving zero critical audit findings and >95% CAPA effectiveness.

**RESP-MF-025: Inspection Readiness & Success** - Achieve inspection success with zero critical 483s and >90% inspector satisfaction.

**RESP-MF-026: Quality Risk Management** - Conduct QRM achieving >90% risk mitigation effectiveness and zero critical risks materialized.

**RESP-MF-027: Manufacturing Planning Accuracy** - Achieve >90% forecast accuracy and >95% on-time campaign execution.

**RESP-MF-028: Supply Chain Reliability** - Maintain >99% supply reliability with zero stockouts and <2% waste.

**RESP-MF-029: Inventory Optimization** - Optimize inventory achieving <60 days on hand while maintaining >99% fill rate.

**RESP-MF-030: CMC Regulatory Approval** - Achieve >90% CMC approval rate with zero manufacturing-related deficiencies.

**RESP-MF-031: Facility Registration Compliance** - Maintain 100% facility registration currency and inspection readiness.

**RESP-MF-032: Post-Approval Change Success** - Execute post-approval changes with >95% approval rate and minimal business impact.
