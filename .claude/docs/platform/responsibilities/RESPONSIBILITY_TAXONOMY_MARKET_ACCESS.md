# Market Access & HEOR Responsibility Taxonomy  
**Version**: 1.0 | **Date**: November 27, 2025 | **Status**: COMPLETE

## Role-Specific Responsibilities (30)

**RESP-MA-001: Market Access Strategy Execution** - Execute global market access strategy achieving >85% formulary access targets across priority markets.

**RESP-MA-002: Payer Engagement Success** - Build relationships with 100% of tier-1 payers and maintain >80% satisfaction scores.

**RESP-MA-003: HEOR Evidence Generation** - Generate health economics evidence supporting >90% of market access objectives with regulatory acceptance.

**RESP-MA-004: Account Management Excellence** - Manage payer accounts achieving >85% contract renewal rate and relationship scores.

**RESP-MA-005: Contract Negotiation Success** - Negotiate contracts achieving >80% favorable outcomes on pricing and formulary placement.

**RESP-MA-006: Formulary Access Achievement** - Achieve formulary placement meeting >85% coverage targets with preferred tier status.

**RESP-MA-007: Reimbursement Strategy Success** - Achieve >90% reimbursement approval rate across target markets and payers.

**RESP-MA-008: Value-Based Contract Implementation** - Implement value-based contracts achieving >80% performance targets and outcomes goals.

**RESP-MA-009: Economic Model Quality** - Deliver economic models with >95% technical validity and regulatory acceptance.

**RESP-MA-010: CEA Regulatory Acceptance** - Achieve >85% CEA acceptance rate by HTA agencies and payers.

**RESP-MA-011: BIA Payer Acceptance** - Deliver budget impact analyses meeting >90% payer acceptance with accurate forecasts (±15%).

**RESP-MA-012: Pharmacoeconomic Model Validity** - Maintain model validity with >95% validation success and peer review acceptance.

**RESP-MA-013: HTA Submission Success** - Achieve >75% positive HTA recommendations (NICE, CADTH, IQWIG, HAS).

**RESP-MA-014: Model Validation Quality** - Validate models achieving >98% technical accuracy and regulatory compliance.

**RESP-MA-015: RWE Strategy Execution** - Execute RWE strategy delivering evidence for >85% of market access claims.

**RESP-MA-016: Observational Study Success** - Deliver RWE studies meeting quality, timeline, and evidence objectives (>90%).

**RESP-MA-017: PRO Validation & Utility** - Validate and analyze PROs supporting >80% of value proposition claims.

**RESP-MA-018: RWD Analysis Quality** - Conduct RWD analyses with >95% methodological rigor and regulatory acceptance.

**RESP-MA-019: CER Credibility** - Deliver comparative effectiveness research with >90% stakeholder credibility and acceptance.

**RESP-MA-020: Global Pricing Strategy Success** - Achieve pricing targets in >85% of launch markets within ±10% of target.

**RESP-MA-021: Value Proposition Impact** - Develop value propositions achieving >80% payer persuasion and formulary success.

**RESP-MA-022: Value Dossier Quality** - Deliver value dossiers meeting >95% quality standards and payer requirements.

**RESP-MA-023: HEOR Communication Effectiveness** - Communicate HEOR data achieving >85% payer understanding and acceptance scores.

**RESP-MA-024: Evidence Gap Closure** - Close evidence gaps for >90% of identified market access barriers.

**RESP-MA-025: Policy Influence Impact** - Influence policies achieving favorable outcomes in >70% of advocacy initiatives.

**RESP-MA-026: Coverage Policy Success** - Achieve positive coverage policies for >80% of targeted indications and populations.

**RESP-MA-027: Patient Access Program Enrollment** - Enroll >90% of eligible patients in access programs with >85% satisfaction.

**RESP-MA-028: Market Access Analytics Insights** - Generate actionable insights for >85% of market access decisions.

**RESP-MA-029: Payer Intelligence Quality** - Deliver payer intelligence with >90% accuracy and strategic utility.

**RESP-MA-030: Access Monitoring & Optimization** - Monitor access achieving >85% pull-through optimization and barrier resolution.
