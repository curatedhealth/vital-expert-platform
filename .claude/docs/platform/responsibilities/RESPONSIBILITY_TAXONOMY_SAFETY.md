## Role-Specific Responsibilities (28)

**RESP-SV-001: Global PV Strategy Execution** - Own global pharmacovigilance strategy ensuring patient safety and regulatory compliance across all products and markets.

**RESP-SV-002: PV Operations Excellence** - Lead PV operations achieving case processing, signal detection, and reporting targets with 100% compliance.

**RESP-SV-003: Medical Safety Leadership** - Provide medical leadership for safety function ensuring clinical oversight and benefit-risk management.

**RESP-SV-004: Case Processing Quality & Timeliness** - Process ICSRs with >98% quality and 100% timeline compliance.

**RESP-SV-005: SAE Assessment Accuracy** - Assess SAEs with 100% accuracy in seriousness, causality, and reportability determinations.

**RESP-SV-006: Expedited Reporting Compliance** - Achieve 100% compliance with expedited reporting timelines (15-day/7-day) with zero late submissions.

**RESP-SV-007: Safety Database Quality** - Maintain safety database with >99% data accuracy and 100% system validation.

**RESP-SV-008: Signal Detection Effectiveness** - Detect safety signals achieving >95% true signal rate and <10% false positive rate.

**RESP-SV-009: Signal Assessment Quality** - Evaluate signals with clinical rigor achieving >90% recommendation acceptance rate.

**RESP-SV-010: RMP/REMS Implementation Success** - Implement risk management plans achieving >95% effectiveness in risk minimization.

**RESP-SV-011: Benefit-Risk Balance Maintenance** - Maintain favorable benefit-risk profile through ongoing assessment and management.

**RESP-SV-012: Safety Committee Effectiveness** - Manage safety committees achieving 100% meeting frequency and >90% recommendation implementation.

**RESP-SV-013: PSUR/PBRER Quality & Timeliness** - Deliver PSURs/PBRERs with 100% regulatory compliance and timeline adherence.

**RESP-SV-014: DSUR Regulatory Compliance** - Submit DSURs meeting 100% ICH E2F requirements and regulatory deadlines.

**RESP-SV-015: Safety Data Analysis Rigor** - Conduct safety analyses with statistical rigor supporting regulatory decisions.

**RESP-SV-016: Aggregate Report Quality** - Author aggregate reports meeting >95% quality standards and regulatory acceptance.

**RESP-SV-017: PV Regulatory Compliance Achievement** - Achieve 100% compliance with all global PV regulations and zero violations.

**RESP-SV-018: Safety Submission Quality** - Submit safety reports with >98% quality and 100% regulatory acceptance.

**RESP-SV-019: PSMF Currency & Compliance** - Maintain PSMF with 100% currency and regulatory compliance.

**RESP-SV-020: Inspection Success Rate** - Achieve >95% inspection success rate with zero critical findings.

**RESP-SV-021: Medical Safety Surveillance Quality** - Provide medical surveillance with 100% case coverage and clinical appropriateness.

**RESP-SV-022: Clinical Trial Safety Oversight** - Ensure clinical trial safety with zero preventable harm and 100% reporting compliance.

**RESP-SV-023: Product Safety Profile Accuracy** - Maintain accurate safety profiles with 100% CDS/RSI currency.

**RESP-SV-024: Literature Surveillance Completeness** - Achieve >98% literature surveillance completeness with zero missed cases.

**RESP-SV-025: Medication Error Management** - Manage medication errors with 100% processing compliance and trend analysis.

**RESP-SV-026: PASS/PAES Delivery** - Deliver post-authorization studies meeting 100% regulatory commitments and timelines.

**RESP-SV-027: Vendor Safety Quality Oversight** - Maintain vendor quality with >95% performance scores and 100% compliance.

**RESP-SV-028: Safety Training & Competency** - Deliver safety training achieving 100% completion and >90% competency scores.
