## üìã Persona Development Template

For each persona, ensure you capture:

### Core Attributes
- **Title** (specific variation)
- **Function** (Business Function)
- **Department**
- **Role** (base role they map to)
- **Seniority Level** (executive, director, senior, mid-level, junior, entry)
- **Career Level** (1-10 scale)

### Contextual Attributes
- **Geographic Scope** (global, regional, local, centralized)
- **Company Size** (large pharma, mid-size, specialty, biotech, startup)
- **Company Type** (innovator, generic, CDMO, etc.)
- **Therapeutic Area Focus** (pan-TA, multi-TA, single-TA)
- **Product Type** (small molecule, biologics, cell & gene, vaccines, etc.)
- **Career Stage** (early career, experienced, expert, transition, leadership)

### Behavioral Attributes
- **Needs & Pain Points** (3-5 key challenges)
- **Motivations & Goals** (what drives them)
- **Decision Authority** (what they can approve)
- **Key Stakeholders** (who they interact with)
- **Technology Proficiency** (digital maturity)
- **AI/ML Readiness** (adoption stage)

### Professional Attributes
- **Typical Team Size** (if manager/leader)
- **Budget Authority** (if applicable)
- **Reporting Structure** (reports to whom)
- **Career Path** (typical progression)
- **KPIs** (how success is measured)
