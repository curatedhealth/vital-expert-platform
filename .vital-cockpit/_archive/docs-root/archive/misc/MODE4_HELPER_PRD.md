## ðŸ“‹ Overview

**What**: An interactive helper component for Mode 4 (Automatic Autonomous) that demonstrates AI orchestration through the metaphor of a musical symphony, showing how multiple experts collaborate seamlessly to create comprehensive solutions.

**Why**: Mode 4 is the most sophisticated mode but users can't see the "intelligence" behind expert selection, switching, and synthesis. They need to understand and trust the AI's orchestration capabilities.

**Who**: Power users tackling complex multi-domain problems, executives needing comprehensive strategies, professionals who want the "best of all experts" working together.

---

## ðŸŽ¨ Core Experience Principles

### 1. **Symphonic Metaphor**
- Position AI as a masterful conductor
- Show experts as specialized musicians
- Demonstrate how individual contributions create beautiful harmony

### 2. **Orchestration Transparency**
- Reveal the intelligence behind expert selection
- Show seamless transitions and context preservation
- Demonstrate synthesis creates coherent masterpieces

### 3. **Confidence Through Visibility**
- Users see the "magic" happening in real-time
- Build trust through process transparency
- Make complexity feel manageable and beautiful

### 4. **Progressive Empowerment**
- Start with inspiration (the symphony vision)
- Build understanding through interactive demos
- End with mastery (users can conduct their own orchestras)
