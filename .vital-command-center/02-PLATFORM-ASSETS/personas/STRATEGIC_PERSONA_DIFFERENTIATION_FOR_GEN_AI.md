# Strategic Persona Differentiation for Gen AI Opportunities

**Date**: 2025-01-27  
**Purpose**: Create 4 distinct personas per role optimized for Gen AI opportunity discovery, JTBD mapping, and AI maturity assessment

---

## üîç Secondary Differentiation Attributes

### **For Enterprise Opportunity Mapping:**

1. **Geographic Scope** (`geographic_scope`)
   - `global`: Enterprise-wide opportunities
   - `regional`: Multi-country opportunities
   - `local`/`country`: Country-specific opportunities
   - **Use for**: Market expansion, localization needs

2. **Organization Size** (`typical_organization_size`)
   - `Large Pharma`: Scale opportunities, standardization
   - `Emerging Biopharma`: Innovation opportunities, agility
   - `Specialty Pharma`: Niche opportunities, precision
   - **Use for**: Account sizing, expansion potential

3. **Seniority Level** (`seniority_level`)
   - `entry`/`mid-level`: Individual productivity gains
   - `senior`/`director`: Team and process improvements
   - `executive`: Strategic transformation opportunities
   - **Use for**: Decision-making authority, budget access

4. **Team Size** (`team_size_typical`, `direct_reports`)
   - Individual contributor: Personal productivity
   - Small team (1-5): Team efficiency
   - Medium team (6-15): Process optimization
   - Large team (16+): Organizational transformation
   - **Use for**: Scale of impact, ROI calculation
