## ðŸ“‹ Overview

**What**: An interactive helper component for Mode 3 (Manual Autonomous) that demonstrates the magic of expert-AI collaboration, showcases workflow capabilities, and builds excitement for complex project execution.

**Why**: Mode 3 is the most powerful but also most complex mode. Users need to understand the expert-AI partnership, workflow planning, and collaborative potential before they can fully leverage it.

**Who**: Power users, project managers, professionals who need systematic approaches to complex problems, users ready for deep expert collaboration.

---

## ðŸŽ¨ Core Experience Principles

### 1. **Collaborative Magic**
- Show the "expert + AI" partnership in action
- Demonstrate seamless human-AI collaboration
- Highlight the unique value of persistent expert relationships

### 2. **Workflow Storytelling**
- Turn complex processes into compelling narratives
- Show transformation from idea to deliverable
- Make planning and execution feel like an adventure

### 3. **Progressive Empowerment**
- Start with inspiration, end with mastery
- Build confidence through interactive examples
- Show users they can tackle complex projects

### 4. **Trust Through Transparency**
- Reveal the planning intelligence behind workflows
- Show checkpoint benefits and user control
- Demonstrate quality through process visibility
