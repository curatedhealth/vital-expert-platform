## Design System & Styling

### Color Palette
- **Primary Blue:** `#1E40AF` (regulatory-blue)
- **Trust Blue:** `#3B82F6` (trust-blue)
- **Clinical Green:** `#10B981` (clinical-green)
- **Progress Teal:** `#14B8A6` (progress-teal)
- **Market Purple:** `#8B5CF6` (market-purple)
- **Data Purple:** `#7C3AED` (data-purple)
- **Vital Black:** `#000000` (vital-black)
- **Vital White:** `#FFFFFF` (vital-white)
- **Gray Scale:** Various shades from `#F9FAFB` to `#111827`

### Typography
- **Headings:** Font-extrabold, large sizes (40px-56px)
- **Body Text:** Font-medium, readable sizes (16px-20px)
- **Small Text:** Font-semibold, smaller sizes (12px-14px)
- **Tracking:** Tight tracking for headlines (-0.02em)

### Spacing & Layout
- **Max Width:** 1200px containers
- **Padding:** 10px horizontal, 20px-100px vertical sections
- **Grid:** Responsive grid layouts (1-4 columns)
- **Gaps:** 4px-12px between elements

### Interactive Elements
- **Hover Effects:** Translate-y, shadow changes, color transitions
- **Transitions:** 200-300ms duration for smooth interactions
- **Buttons:** Rounded corners (8px-12px), padding (8px-16px)
- **Cards:** Subtle shadows, hover lift effects
