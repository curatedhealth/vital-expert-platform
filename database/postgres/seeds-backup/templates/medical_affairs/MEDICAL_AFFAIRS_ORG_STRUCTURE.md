## üè• Medical Affairs Workflows & Processes

### **Critical Processes (Map roles to RACI)**

1. **Adverse Event Reporting**
   - Trigger: AE identified in field or via inquiry
   - Timeline: 24 hours to safety database
   - Roles: MSL (R), MI Specialist (R), Safety (A)

2. **Medical Information Response**
   - Trigger: Unsolicited inquiry from HCP
   - Timeline: 24-48 hours
   - Roles: MI Specialist (R), Medical Director (A)

3. **Medical Review of Promotional Materials**
   - Trigger: Marketing creates new material
   - Timeline: 5-7 business days
   - Roles: Medical Director (R/A), Regulatory (C)

4. **KOL Engagement Planning**
   - Trigger: Quarterly planning cycle
   - Timeline: 2 weeks
   - Roles: MSL (R), MSL Manager (A), Medical Director (I)

5. **Publication Planning**
   - Trigger: Clinical data readout
   - Timeline: 6-12 months to publication
   - Roles: Medical Writer (R), Medical Director (A), MSL (C)

6. **Advisory Board Execution**
   - Trigger: Strategic need or launch preparation
   - Timeline: 3-4 months planning
   - Roles: Medical Director (A), MSL (R), Medical Ops (C)

7. **Congress Planning & Attendance**
   - Trigger: Annual congress calendar
   - Timeline: 6 months advance planning
   - Roles: MSL (R), MSL Manager (A), Medical Ops (C)

## üîç Medical Affairs Role Characteristics

### **Common Attributes Across MA Roles**

**Work Environment:**
- High travel (MSLs: 50-70%, Directors: 30-40%)
- Remote-eligible (most roles)
- Flexible schedule (to accommodate HCP availability)
- Congress attendance (4-6 major congresses/year)

**Compliance Context:**
- ALL roles are GxP-critical
- Extensive documentation requirements
- Firewalls from Commercial (promotional vs. medical)
- Transparency reporting (Sunshine Act)

**Career Paths:**
- Entry: Post-doctoral fellow, Clinical Research
- Mid: MSL, MI Specialist, Medical Writer
- Senior: Senior MSL, Senior MI Specialist
- Management: MSL Manager, MI Manager
- Leadership: Medical Director, VP Medical Affairs

**Motivations:**
- Patient impact (evidence-based medicine)
- Scientific excellence and credibility
- Thought leader relationships
- Cutting-edge therapeutic knowledge
- Work-life balance (vs. clinical practice)
